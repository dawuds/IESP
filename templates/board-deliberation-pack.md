## 1. Executive Summary

### 1.1 Purpose

This paper presents the results of the Independent External Service Provider (IESP) assessment of [subject matter -- e.g., the FI's data centre resilience / network resilience / cloud services / AI deployment / system implementation] for the Board Committee's deliberation, in accordance with paragraph 8.3 of the RMIT (November 2025).

### 1.2 Regulatory Basis

Under paragraph 8.3 of the RMIT, the Board and senior management are required to deliberate on the findings and recommendations of IESP assessments. The designated Board-level committee is responsible for:

- Reviewing and deliberating on the IESP assessment results
- Ensuring that identified risks are adequately addressed
- Approving remediation plans and timelines
- Monitoring the implementation of remediation actions
