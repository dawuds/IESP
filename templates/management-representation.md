Dear [ESP Engagement Lead Name],

This management representation letter is provided in connection with your assessment of [description of the subject matter] performed pursuant to the Risk Management in Technology (RMIT) Policy Document (November 2025) issued by Bank Negara Malaysia (BNM).

We understand that the purpose of your assessment is to provide assurance on the effectiveness of our technology risk management controls against the requirements of the RMIT, and to issue a risk assessment report in the format prescribed under Appendix 7 Part A.

We confirm the following representations to the best of our knowledge and belief:

---

### General Representations

1. **Responsibility for controls.** We acknowledge that the design, implementation, and maintenance of effective technology risk management controls is the responsibility of the management of [Financial Institution Name]. Your assessment does not relieve us of this responsibility.

2. **Completeness of information.** We have provided you with all information, documentation, and access to systems, premises, and personnel that you have requested and that we consider relevant to the scope of your assessment. We are not aware of any material information that has been withheld.

3. **Accuracy of information.** To the best of our knowledge, the information, documentation, and explanations provided to you are complete, accurate, and not misleading. Where estimates or judgements have been made, these have been made on a reasonable basis.

4. **Disclosure of matters.** We have disclosed to you all matters that we consider may be relevant to your assessment, including but not limited to:
   - All known instances of non-compliance with the RMIT or other applicable regulations relating to technology risk management
   - All known security incidents, breaches, or near-misses that occurred during or after the assessment period
   - All known material weaknesses in internal controls relating to the systems and processes within the scope of your assessment
   - All known fraud or suspected fraud involving management or employees with significant roles in technology risk management
   - All prior audit or assessment findings (internal and external) relating to the systems and processes within the scope of your assessment

5. **Currency of documentation.** The policies, procedures, standards, and other documentation provided to you are the current, approved versions in effect during the assessment period. Where documentation has been updated during the assessment period, we have informed you of such updates.

---

### Technology-Specific Representations

6. **System inventory.** The system and infrastructure inventory provided to you is complete and accurate as at [date]. There are no material systems, applications, or infrastructure components within the scope of your assessment that have not been disclosed.

7. **Architecture documentation.** The architecture diagrams, network diagrams, and system documentation provided to you accurately represent the current state of the technology environment within the scope of your assessment.

8. **Configuration information.** The configuration information, settings, and parameters provided to you or made available for your review accurately reflect the current production environment.

9. **Access controls.** The access control lists, user access matrices, and privileged access inventories provided to you are complete and current as at [date].

10. **Change management.** All material changes to the systems and infrastructure within the scope of your assessment during the assessment period have been disclosed and were subject to the FI's change management process.

11. **Incident history.** We have disclosed all technology incidents, security breaches, and service disruptions affecting the systems within the scope of your assessment that occurred during [relevant period -- e.g., the past 12 months].

12. **Third-party arrangements.** We have disclosed all material third-party and outsourcing arrangements relating to the systems and infrastructure within the scope of your assessment, including cloud service provider arrangements.

13. **Regulatory matters.** We have disclosed all regulatory observations, directions, or enforcement actions by BNM or other regulators relating to the systems and processes within the scope of your assessment.

---

### Representations Specific to the Engagement Type

*[Select and include the relevant section(s) below based on the engagement type.]*

#### For Data Centre Resilience Assessment (DCRA)

14. We have disclosed all data centre facilities (primary, disaster recovery, and near-site) that support the critical systems within the scope of this assessment.

15. The physical security controls, environmental monitoring systems, and fire suppression systems in the data centre facilities are operational and have been maintained in accordance with manufacturer and regulatory requirements.

16. Business continuity and disaster recovery plans for the data centre facilities are current and have been tested within the timeframe stated in the documentation provided.

#### For Network Resilience Assessment (NRA)

17. The network architecture diagrams provided accurately represent the current network topology, including all segments, zones, and interconnections.

18. We have disclosed all external network connections, third-party network links, and internet-facing services.

19. The network monitoring and security tools identified in the documentation provided are operational and configured as described.

#### For Cloud Services Assessment

20. We have disclosed all cloud service arrangements, including the cloud service providers, service models (IaaS/PaaS/SaaS), deployment models, and data locations.

21. All cloud service provider contracts, SLAs, and third-party assurance reports (SOC 1, SOC 2, ISO 27001, etc.) relevant to the scope have been provided.

22. We have disclosed any data sovereignty, cross-border, or regulatory concerns relating to the cloud services within scope.

#### For AI / Emerging Technology Assessment

23. We have disclosed all AI and emerging technology deployments within the scope of this assessment, including their use cases, data sources, and decision-making authority.

24. The governance framework, risk assessments, and acceptance criteria for the AI/emerging technology deployments have been provided.

25. We have disclosed any known issues relating to bias, fairness, explainability, or adverse outcomes from the AI/emerging technology deployments.

### Subsequent Events

26. We will inform you of any events or changes occurring between the date of this letter and the date of your final report that may have a material effect on the systems, controls, or findings within the scope of your assessment.

### Acknowledgement

27. We acknowledge that your assessment is conducted in accordance with the scope and methodology agreed in the engagement letter dated [date] and the scoping document dated [date]. Your assessment provides reasonable assurance and is subject to inherent limitations.

28. We acknowledge that your risk assessment report will be provided to the Board / designated Board committee and may be provided to BNM as required under the RMIT.
