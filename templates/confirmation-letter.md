**Re: Confirmation Letter pursuant to Appendix 7 Part B of the Risk Management in Technology Policy Document (November 2025)**

**Subject:** [Name of system/service/infrastructure under assessment]

---

Dear Sir/Madam,

In connection with the [implementation / continued operation / material change] of [name and description of the system/service/infrastructure], and pursuant to the requirements of the Risk Management in Technology (RMIT) Policy Document (November 2025), the Board of Directors / Senior Management of [Financial Institution Name] hereby confirms the following:

---

### 1. Consistency with Strategic and Business Plans

We confirm that the [system/service/infrastructure] is consistent with the financial institution's strategic and business plans.

**Supporting details:**

- The [system/service/infrastructure] is aligned with the FI's [strategic plan name/reference, dated XX/XX/XXXX].
- The business case for the [system/service/infrastructure] was approved by [approving authority] on [date].
- The [system/service/infrastructure] supports the following strategic objectives: [list key objectives].

---

### 2. Board/Senior Management Understanding of RMIT Responsibilities

We confirm that the Board of Directors and senior management understand their responsibilities under the RMIT in relation to the [system/service/infrastructure].

**Supporting details:**

- The Board / Board Risk Committee / Board Technology Committee has been briefed on the RMIT requirements applicable to the [system/service/infrastructure] on [date(s)].
- Roles and responsibilities for technology risk management have been clearly defined and communicated.
- The designated committee responsible for oversight of technology risk is [name of committee].

---

### 3. Risk Management Process Subject to Appropriate Oversight

We confirm that the risk management process in respect of the [system/service/infrastructure] is subject to appropriate oversight.

**Supporting details:**

- A technology risk assessment has been conducted by [ESP Name] as an Independent External Service Provider (IESP).
- The risk assessment report (dated [date], reference [reference number]) has been reviewed by [name of committee/management].
- Ongoing risk monitoring and reporting mechanisms are in place, including [describe mechanisms].
- The risk management process is governed by the FI's [relevant policy/framework name].

---

### 4. Appropriate Security Measures in Place

We confirm that appropriate security measures are in place for the [system/service/infrastructure].

**Supporting details:**

- Security controls have been designed and implemented in accordance with the FI's [information security policy name/reference].
- The controls address, at minimum, the requirements of the RMIT, including [list key areas -- e.g., access controls, encryption, network security, vulnerability management].
- Security testing, including [vulnerability assessment / penetration testing / security review], has been performed by [party] with results dated [date].
- Any findings from security assessments have been addressed or have approved remediation plans in place.

---

### 5. Customer Support Services in Place

We confirm that adequate customer support services are in place for the [system/service/infrastructure].

**Supporting details:**

- Customer support channels include [list channels -- e.g., call centre, online banking support, branch support].
- Service level targets for customer support have been established, including [list key SLAs -- e.g., response time, resolution time].
- Customer complaint handling procedures are in place and address technology-related service disruptions.
- Customer communication plans are in place for [planned downtime / service disruptions / incident notifications].

---

### 6. Performance Monitoring Established

We confirm that performance monitoring has been established for the [system/service/infrastructure].

**Supporting details:**

- Key performance indicators (KPIs) and service level agreements (SLAs) have been defined, including [list key metrics -- e.g., system availability, response time, throughput].
- Monitoring tools and processes are in place to track performance against established targets.
- Performance reports are generated [frequency] and reviewed by [responsible party/committee].
- Escalation procedures are in place for performance threshold breaches.

---

### 7. Contingency and Business Resumption Plans

We confirm that contingency and business resumption plans are in place for the [system/service/infrastructure].

**Supporting details:**

- A Business Continuity Plan (BCP) covering the [system/service/infrastructure] has been developed and approved by [approving authority] on [date].
- A Disaster Recovery Plan (DRP) has been established with a Recovery Time Objective (RTO) of [X hours/minutes] and Recovery Point Objective (RPO) of [X hours/minutes].
- DR testing was last conducted on [date] with [successful / partially successful] results.
- The BCP/DRP is reviewed and updated [frequency -- e.g., annually, or upon material change].

---

### 8. Adequate Resources

We confirm that adequate resources have been allocated for the [system/service/infrastructure].

**Supporting details:**

- Sufficient human resources with the required skills and competencies have been assigned to manage and operate the [system/service/infrastructure].
- Key roles include [list key roles and staffing levels].
- Training programmes are in place to maintain and enhance staff competencies.
- Adequate financial resources have been budgeted for ongoing operation, maintenance, and enhancement.
- Vendor/third-party support arrangements are in place as needed, including [list key arrangements].

---

### 9. Systems, Procedures, and Security Measures Will Be Constantly Reviewed

We confirm that the systems, procedures, and security measures for the [system/service/infrastructure] will be constantly reviewed.

**Supporting details:**

- A periodic review schedule has been established, including:
  - Security reviews: [frequency]
  - Vulnerability assessments: [frequency]
  - Penetration testing: [frequency]
  - Policy and procedure reviews: [frequency]
  - Technology risk assessments: [frequency]
- Change management procedures are in place to assess the impact of changes on the security and risk posture.
- Continuous monitoring mechanisms include [list -- e.g., SIEM, log monitoring, threat intelligence feeds].
- The FI will engage an IESP for subsequent assessments in accordance with the RMIT requirements.

---

### Declaration

We declare that the information provided in this confirmation letter is true and accurate to the best of our knowledge and belief.

We acknowledge that this confirmation letter is provided to Bank Negara Malaysia pursuant to the RMIT Policy Document (November 2025) and that any material misstatement may constitute a breach of regulatory requirements.
