This checklist maps evidence requirements to the NRA assessment areas based on RMIT paragraphs 10.36-10.43. For each item:

- **Source:** FI = Financial Institution, VEN = Vendor/Service Provider, ESP = generated by the ESP during assessment
- **Format:** Expected format of the evidence
- **Criticality:** Required = must be obtained; Desirable = should be obtained if available
