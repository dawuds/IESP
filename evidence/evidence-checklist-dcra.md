## Instructions

This checklist maps evidence requirements to the DCRA Audit Work Programme (AWP) test steps. For each item:

- **Source:** Who provides the evidence (FI = Financial Institution, DC = Data Centre Operator, ESP = generated by the ESP during assessment)
- **Format:** Expected format of the evidence
- **Criticality:** Required = must be obtained; Desirable = should be obtained if available
- **Status:** Track receipt and review
